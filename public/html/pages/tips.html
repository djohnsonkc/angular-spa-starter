Tips

These are a list of tips for optimizing your site.

Prioritize visible content (aka "above the fold" content)

This site offers some good guidance on how to prioritize the visible content (aka "above the fold" content) of your site so that a user doesn't have to wait for non-essential portions of your page to load.

https://www.feedthebot.com/pagespeed/prioritize-visible-content.html

What we did

This trick increased our Google Developers PageSpeed Insights ranking from a 79/100 to a 91/100 with one simple change. In our index.html page, we removed the link to our single CSS file and pasted the contents of our /public/css/dist/app.min.css directly into the head of our index.html page. This elminated the need for a user's browser to load the separate CSS file before rending the page.
